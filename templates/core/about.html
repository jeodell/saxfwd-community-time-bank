About Our Time Bank

Building a stronger, more connected community through the exchange of time and skills

Our Mission

The Saxapahaw Timebank exists to strengthen our community by facilitating the exchange of skills, services, and time between neighbors. We believe that everyone has something valuable to contribute, and that by working together, we can create a more resilient and connected community.

Our goal is to build a network of trust and reciprocity where time is our currency, and every hour of service is valued equally.

How Time Banking Works

1️⃣

Join the Community

Create your account and tell us about the skills and services you can offer to your neighbors.

2️⃣

Offer Your Skills

List the services you can provide, from gardening to tech support, home repairs to childcare.

3️⃣

Earn Time Credits

For every hour you help someone, you earn one time credit that you can use to receive help from others.

4️⃣

Build Connections

Meet your neighbors, share skills, and strengthen the bonds within our community.

Our Core Values

Equality

Every hour of service is valued equally, regardless of the type of service provided. An hour of gardening is worth the same as an hour of legal advice.

Reciprocity

We believe in giving and receiving in equal measure. The time bank system ensures that everyone's contributions are recognized and valued.

Community

We're building a network of trust and support where neighbors help neighbors and everyone's skills are valued.

Respect

We treat all members with respect and dignity, recognizing that everyone has something valuable to contribute.
