About

In a Timebank, people exchange time instead of money. One hour of service earns one credit, which can be spent by receiving help from another member.

Our Mission

Saxapahaw's mission is to create social trust and collaboration through skill-sharing and mutual support. We believe everyone has something valuable to offer, and together we can build a resilient, connected community.

Our Values

In a Timebank, everyone's time has the same value. The power of community comes from recognizing and supporting every member's contribution.
